Audience Test Questions

Tasks: Narrate out loud as you complete these


  1. Explore the home page and find a way to set a goal and submit it.
  2. Write a daily report.
  3. Try and view your stats. Find all the places where you can see them.

Questions:


  1. Can you walk me through setting a task and describe out loud what you are thinking as you do it?
  2. Can you describe what was the most frustrating thing to find on this website?
  3. How often would you use this app?
  4. Do you think this is a feasible way to form habits? If not, why?
  5. What blocks can you foresee users having as they use this product?

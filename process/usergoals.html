Comparative Analysis

User Goals


How will your users understand the content of the project?

The users will understand the content of the project through a small onboarding process when first using the app, as well as optional tool tips throughout the interface during use.


How will your users understand the primary objectives?

The primary objectives will be laid out by the users themselves, since they will be the ones setting their own goals.


What content and features do your users need, and in what form do they need it?

Users need forms to submit their goals and features to help keep track of their goals once they are established. They need data visuals that will succinctly tell them information about their actions at a glance as well as prompts that incdicate future steps after the stats are given.


How do your users think your interactive projects should work and what are their experience levels with the type of media you or your team is creating?

The users should understand the basic form and will be technologically aware enough to navigate the different GUI elements. Presented together as a whole, information will be grouped on different screens with guiding text to help the user move through the app.
